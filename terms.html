Description of Service

Cadence is a free, browser-based personal health logging application. Cadence allows you to:

  • Log daily health entries (symptoms, medications, notes) in your browser
  • View summaries and insights based on your entries
  • Optionally sync your data to a Google Sheet you own
Cadence does not maintain its own database or server-side storage of user health data. All health data is stored locally in your browser or in a Google Sheet you choose to connect.

Cadence is provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis. We do not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free operation of the Service.
